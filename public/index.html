How to Play

Your goal is to guess the mystery movie chosen from the current top 1000 highest grossing movies within 10 tries

Guessing

When you submit a guess, you will notice you get some feedback. This will show you what matches the mystery movie from your guess

Feedback System

Year

This will highlight in ORANGE if it is within 5 years of the mystery movie's release date

Genre

This will highlight in ORANGE if at least 1 of the genres match with the mystery movie. Any genres that match exactly will highlight in GREEN.

The available genres are: Action, Adventure, Animation, Fantasy, Drama, Romance, Sci-Fi, Thriller, Horror, Crime, Comedy, History, Family, Musical, Mystery, Western, Documentary, and Sport

Director

This will highlight in GREEN if it matches. Some films have several directors, this will just be displayed as "Multiple Directors"

Age Rating

This will highlight in GREEN if it matches. The available age ratings are: PG, PG-13, R, TV-MA, Passed, and Not Rated

Duration

This will highlight in ORANGE if it is within 15 minutes of the mystery movie's duration
